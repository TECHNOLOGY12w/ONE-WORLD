Who are we

About Us:OisD is a non-governmental, non- partisan, non- profit making organisation conceptualized to galvanize Skakeholder effort towards promoting the culture of peace through dynamic conflict transformation strategies for poverty reduction and sustainable development in Nigeria and Africa in general. The One World Initiative for Sustainable Development is committed to fostering global awareness and creating impactful solutions to pressing issues such as poverty, climate change, gender equality, and access to education. Our organization works with communities, governments, and international partners to ensure that no one is left behind in the global movement for sustainability. We believe in a united world where collaboration, innovation, and compassion lead to a brighter, greener, and more equitable future for all.

Our Mission: Intervention We intervene in areas affected by poverty, climate change, and injustice to provide immediate support and long-term solutions. Advocacy We advocate for policies that promote sustainable development and ensure equity in opportunities for marginalized groups.
